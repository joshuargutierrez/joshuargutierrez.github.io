  • 1. Introduction:

    1.1 Purpose:

    Custom Hero RPG Survival is a wave based survival, upgrade your hero, and
    try different builds each game. Not for the faint of heart!

    1.2 Scope:

    The player can choose from several different heros, different abilites,
    different weapons, and attempt to survive, or at least outlive their opponents.

    1.3 Acronyms, Abbreviations, Definitions:

    Talent - An inherent hero ability (unique for each hero).

    1.4 References:

    N/A

    2. General Description:

    2.2 Context:

    This game has been developed using Unity 2019, is built specifically for PC gaming.

    2.2 Domain Model:

    Characters will have abilities, can hold weapons, have inherent talents, and
    cannot respawn on death (unless they have reincarnation-ability).
    Waves will be based on the amount of players, the level the players are on, and
    will allow players ot purchase items/abilities between waves.

    Players PC must be DX10, DX11, DX12 capable.

    Windows 7 (SP1+) and Windows 10 Xbox One, Hololens Sierra 10.12+
    Ubuntu 16.04 and Ubuntu 18.04 Operating Systems are acceptable
